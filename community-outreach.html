Projects

Medical camp outreach

Free medical camps brought care directly into the villages.

We hosted free medical camps across multiple villages, bringing licensed professionals and volunteers into communities that had little access to regular care. Medicines, check-ups, and direct attention were provided to those who came — and to those who could not.

Beyond the practical care, each camp created space for prayer, conversation, and Gospel witness. It was not a programme disconnected from the mission. It was the mission — carried into the village in a form people could see and receive.

Church construction support

A forest-area church received roofing support to move toward completion.

A local church community had built a frame structure in a forested area using materials from the land around them. What they lacked was roofing — and the means to finish what they had started.

We supplied the roofing materials while the church took responsibility for labour costs. That partnership mattered. The project moved forward without removing ownership from the community it belonged to.

The roof is now complete. The church community continues working on the remaining side structures to bring the building to full use.

Church interior completion

Interior finishing work completed for a local church.

One church had a structure standing but needed support to finish the inside — flooring and essential finishing work that would make the space fully usable for the congregation.

We provided the materials and resources needed to see the work through. The interior is now complete, and the church is able to gather and worship in a space that is finished and fit for the community it serves.

How the work is structured

A simple structure behind the work.

The process matters because it helps explain how support stays careful and close to the field.

1

Discernment through relationship and prayer

We look for pastors faithfully serving in unreached places. Before anyone joins the mission, we spend time with them, observe their work firsthand, travel alongside them, and prayerfully discern God's leading.

2

Partnership through support and encouragement

When a pastor's ministry aligns with the mission, we come alongside them with ongoing financial support, encouragement, and the assurance that they are not serving alone.

3

Serving communities through local churches

Working alongside local pastors, we seek to meet practical and spiritual needs within their communities, demonstrating the love of Christ through service, care, and Gospel witness.

4

Walking together with accountability

We remain closely involved in the work, regularly visiting, encouraging, and discerning alongside each pastor. Rather than leading from a distance, we walk with them in the field, helping ensure the mission remains healthy, faithful, and fruitful.

What we are watching next

The next season is about careful continuation.

We are watching where the work should deepen next, what churches need most, and where God may be opening the next step.

  • Additional pastors We are continuing to discern which pastors should be included as the work expands carefully.
  • Strengthening churches We are paying closer attention to what churches need so they can stand stronger and keep spreading the gospel faithfully.
  • Village care We are watching for practical ways to keep serving communities as the churches grow stronger.
  • Prayer for next steps We want every next move to come from clear prayer and discernment rather than pressure or speed.
