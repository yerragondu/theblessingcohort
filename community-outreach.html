Community outreach

From strengthened churches into the life of the community.

Community outreach is part of the mission flow itself. As pastors are supported and churches grow stronger, the work naturally moves outward into villages through care, presence, and Gospel witness.

See media and photos
Outreach focus
  • Medical camps Serving villages through practical care and licensed medical professionals.
  • Village presence Going beyond towns and staying near rural, remote, and unreached places, while working carefully through real village needs as they come into view.
  • Gospel witness Sharing the Gospel and showing the love of God through prayer, care, faithful presence, and by looking for the most possible ways to reach people well.
Why outreach matters

The mission does not stop at the pastor. It moves into the village.

Our mission statement already gives the order: support pastors, strengthen the church, and from there step into communities with practical help and spiritual witness.

Outreach grows out of church strength.

We do not treat community outreach like a separate side project. It grows out of local pastors and local churches becoming stronger, more stable, and more ready to serve the people around them.

That is why camps, visits, prayer, and village care belong inside the mission itself. They are practical expressions of the same Gospel work already happening through the church.
