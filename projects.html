Medical camp outreach

Medical camps were hosted in villages that needed care.

One of the clearest past projects has been the medical camp outreach carried into multiple villages. These were not separated from the mission itself. They grew out of our desire to serve communities in practical ways while staying close to the church and the Gospel.

Through these camps, villagers received medicines, medical attention, and direct care from licensed professionals and volunteers who were willing to serve in person.

The result was not only practical relief. It also opened space for conversation, prayer, encouragement, and a stronger witness inside the community.

Church construction support

A church structure was set up by the local community in a forest area using trees from the surrounding place to form the main frame. At that stage, the structure required roofing materials and transportation support to move toward completion.

We helped provide the necessary materials for the roofing work while asking the church community to take responsibility for the worker wages. This allowed the project to move forward while maintaining local ownership of the work.

The roofing has now been completed, and the remaining major work is largely finished. The church community is currently working on the side structures and final details to make the building fully complete and usable.

Pastoral Growth & Discernment

We are stepping forward in faith to expand the number of supported pastors, trusting God to raise and establish the right leaders. Our goal is to reach 15 pastors by the end of April and 25 by the end of May.
